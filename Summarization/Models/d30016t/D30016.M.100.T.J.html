[1] Swissair announced that it was disconnecting the Interactive Flight Technologies entertainment system [2] installed on its remaining MD-11s and Boeing 747s [3] after investigators of the crash of Swissair Flight 111 found [4] that the wires of the state-of-the- art system were heavily heat damaged . [5] The flight , bound for Geneva from New York , [6] crashed off the coast of Nova Scotia , [7] killing all 229 people onboard . [8] Swissair is among the carriers [9] that has opted out of a $ 75,000 liability cap for people killed or injured during international flights [10] and is looking into making payments of up to $ 137,000 to the families of the victims .
