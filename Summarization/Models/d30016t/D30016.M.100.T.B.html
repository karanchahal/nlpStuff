[1] All 229 people aboard Swissair 's New York-to-Geneva flight 111 died [2] when the MD-11 airliner plunged into the sea off the Nova Scotia coast on September 2 , 1998 . [3] Pilots reported smoke in the cockpit 16 minutes before the crash . [4] The specific cause of the tragedy was never determined , [5] but suspicions are that an electrical short caused a fire . [6] Following the crash , Swissair voluntarily disconnected its video-on-demand systems [7] because they were connected to a power supply routed through the cockpit . [8] The FAA ordered inspection of two cockpit switches in all MD-11 's [9] and is considering whether there should be a life expectancy for some airplane wiring .
