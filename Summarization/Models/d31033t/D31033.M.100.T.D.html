[1] The Justice department invoked the Sherman Antitrust Act , [2] and brought civil suit against Microsoft , the world 's largest software producer . [3] Twenty states also charged Microsoft . [4] The trial began 23 October 1998 . [5] The government charged that Microsoft sought Netscape Corporation 's collusion in restricting competition in the Internet software market . [6] When Netscape refused , Microsoft attempted to thwart competition by bundling its browser with the highly popular Windows operating system . [7] Microsoft denied all charges . [8] The case is important . [9] Microsoft 's loss could lead to increased watchdog scrutiny in the high-tech world . [10] Microsoft 's attempt to acquire 3Com 's Palm Pilot indicated that their aggressive business practices would continue .
