[1] Results of the 1998 U.S. Mid-Term Congressional elections will be far-reaching . [2] Republicans have a long shot at gaining a filibuster-proof 60-seat majority in the Senate . [3] Noted battles for Senator include D' Amato vs. Schumer in New York , and Moseley-Braun vs. Fitzgerald in Illinois . [4] Republicans are often characterized as `` conservatives '' [5] while Democrats are characterized as `` liberals . '' [6] Political hybrids resist easy characterization [7] as exemplified in D' Amato vs. Schumer . [8] As Election Day nears , the House Judiciary Committee is considering impeaching President Clinton for alleged perjury and obstruction of justice in connection with the Lewinsky affair . [9] The outcome of the elections is likely to influence deliberations about impeaching Clinton .
