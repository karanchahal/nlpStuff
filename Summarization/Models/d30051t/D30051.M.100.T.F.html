[1] Violence continues in Yugoslavia [2] as the International Court in the Hague pushes to have inspectors return [3] to investigate instances of war crimes . [4] President Milosevic continues to deny war crimes even amid reports of a massacre , [5] described by a survivor , and murders by Serbian police in Kosovo . [6] The General Assembly voted to add three judges to the war crimes tribunal , [7] which scored its first guilty verdicts , one of a Serb [8] who confessed to killing fourteen . [9] The war crimes tribunal [10] branded Yugoslavia a `` rogue state '' for denying entry to inspectors but , later , an agreement was reached to allow inspectors to return in November .
