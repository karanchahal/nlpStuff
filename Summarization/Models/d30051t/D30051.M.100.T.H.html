[1] The international war crimes tribunal in The Hague considered including Kosovo in its investigation of Yugoslavia . [2] Milosevic denounced the tribunal , [3] saying no crimes occurred . [4] Investigators left without visiting Kosovo or interviewing a lone massacre survivor . [5] Milosevic agreed to comply but reneged . [6] The UN ruled investigators had authority throughout Yugoslavia , [7] called a `` rogue state '' for refusing Kosovo access . [8] Sanctions were threatened . [9] Three judges and a new trial chamber were added [10] and crimes on both sides investigated . [11] Bosnian Muslim guards at Celebici prison camp were charged with murdering , torturing , raping and beating Serbian prisoners . [12] A Bosnian Serb pleaded guilty to 14 murders , the tribunal 's third conviction . [13] Pre-verdict sentencing arguments speeded tribunal work .
