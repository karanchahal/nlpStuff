[1] The Leonid meteor shower that occurred on 16 and 17 November 1998 consisted of small fragments from the Comet Temple-Tuttle . [2] Some scientists predicted that the 1998 meteor shower would be intense . [3] Asia was expected to be the best viewing area . [4] The display in Asia was disappointing , [5] but was unexpectedly good over southern Europe . [6] Chances of severe damage to any private or government were considered minimal . [7] Nevertheless , operators of the 600 plus satellites that relay national defense information , entertainment and news stories took cautionary steps [8] to further reduce risk . [9] There were no reports of damage to any satellite .
