[1] Unknown Chechen gunmen abducted three Britons and one New Zealander , all employees of a British telephone company in the capitol city of Grozny in Chechnya . [2] The British Foreign Office said it had warned the company to withdraw from Chechnya . [3] More than 100 people are being held captive [4] in Chechnya and anti-kidnapping officials have become victims of apparent hostage takers . [5] Some two months after the kidnapping , [6] Chechen officials reported finding the severed heads of the four kidnapped men near a small village west of Grozny . [7] The Chechen president said the bodies of the four men were recovered [8] but there was no further comment .
