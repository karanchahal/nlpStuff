[1] Three British and one New Zealand telecom workers were kidnapped [2] after a shootout between their bodyguards and the attackers . [3] One bodyguard and an unknown number of attackers were wounded in the abduction in the breakaway Chechnya region . [4] Kidnapping , generally for ransom , has become a growth industry for Chechen rebels with 176 abducted this year . [5] Most kidnappings are for ransom and 90 of the victims have been released . [6] The four foreigners were not so fortunate ; [7] their severed heads were found two months later after a lengthy search . [8] Chechen authorities called the deaths a major blow to efforts for independence [9] and blamed `` outside forces '' and their Chechen henchmen .
