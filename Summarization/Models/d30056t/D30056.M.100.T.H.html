[1] At 4am October 3 , 1998 , gunmen abducted Britons Peter Kennedy , Darren Hickey , Rudolf Petschi , and New Zealander Stanley Shaw from their house in Chechnya . [2] They worked for British firm Granger Telecom installing a telecommunications system . [3] Britain had advised citizens to avoid Chechnya [4] but the Chechen government guaranteed Granger worker safety . [5] Employees went voluntarily . [6] No ransom demand was received . [7] Chechnya 's mountains were thoroughly searched . [8] A captured kidnapper revealed the hostages ' locations . [9] A rescue attempt was mounted but failed . [10] Two months after abduction the hostages ' bodies and severed heads were found . [11] The deaths damaged international recognition of Chechnya 's independence . [12] Many factions were suspected of the kidnappings [13] but no sure blame assigned .
