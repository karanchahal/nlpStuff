[1] Four employees of Grange Telecom , a British telecommunications company , were found murdered following a failed rescue attempt by Chechen authorities . [2] The men had been decapitated . [3] The men , Britons Peter Kennedy , Darren Hickey , and Rudolf Petschi , and New Zealander Stanley Shaw , were abducted during a shootout in October [4] which also resulted in the wounding of several bodyguards . [5] The men were in Chechnya [6] working on installing telecommunications equipment for the Chechen government . [7] Despite warnings from the British government about the dangers of traveling in Chechnya , [8] the company felt the risk to its workers was low [9] because the men were working for the Chechen government .
