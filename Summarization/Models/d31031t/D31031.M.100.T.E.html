[1] Congressional work on the budget was marked along party lines [2] as Democrats sought to include more funding for social and environmental issues [3] while the Republicans sought to push tax cut issues as well as seeking changes to the IMF . [4] When Congress failed to adopt a budget by the end of the fiscal year , [5] it voted a continuing spending resolution [6] to keep the government operating . [7] As talks bogged down , the threat of another government shutdown began to loom . [8] Republicans , wanting to keep public focus on the Clinton impeachment proceedings , [9] backed away from some of the more unpopular riders on the bills , especially some anti-environment issues .
