[1] Syria acceded to Turkish demands [2] that it stop supporting the rebellious PKK [3] and expel the group 's leader , Abdullah Ocalan , averting a military crisis . [4] Turkey calls the PKK , which seeks an autonomous Kurdish state in Turkey , a terrorist organization and Ocalan the country 's most wanted criminal . [5] Nearly 37,000 people have died in the conflict . [6] Ocalan , the subject of a Turkish international arrest warrant , was detained in Rome after arriving there from Moscow [7] where he had sought asylum . [8] It is uncertain whether Italy will agree to Turkey 's extradition request . [9] Meanwhile , hunger strikes to protest Ocalan 's detainment have occurred in Nicosia and Armenia .
