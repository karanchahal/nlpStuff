[1] Abdullah Ocalan , the leader of the Kurdish Workers Party [2] ( PKK ) , was detained on November 12 , 1998 , in Rome [3] as he got off of a flight from Moscow . [4] Turkey , where he is on trial in absentia , is seeking his extradition . [5] Germany also has issued an arrest warrant . [6] The PKK has been fighting Turkey for autonomy since 1984 . [7] After heavy Turkish pressure , Syria had expelled Ocalan on October 21 . [8] Although his location was not known for several weeks , [9] he sought political asylum in Russia . [10] He is now expected to ask for asylum in Italy . [11] Ocalan 's odyssey has spawned demonstrations in Armenia and Cyprus .
