[1] Abdullah Ocalan 's Kurdish Workers Party fought for autonomy in Turkey since 1984 , causing 37,000 deaths . [2] Turkey and the US labeled him a terrorist . [3] Turkey threatened to attack Syria unless Ocalan was expelled . [4] He fled to Russia and requested asylum . [5] Russia 's parliament unanimously asked Yeltsin to grant it , while denying Ocalan 's presence . [6] Turkey asked Russia to extradite him . [7] Greek and Italian parliaments invited him to visit . [8] Kurds in Armenia and Cyprus staged hunger strikes and demonstrated in his support . [9] Ocalan flew from Moscow to Rome under a false passport . [10] Italy had to arrest him on landing , given the serious charges . [11] Ocalan requested Italian asylum . [12] Italy has refused extraditions to capital punishment countries .
