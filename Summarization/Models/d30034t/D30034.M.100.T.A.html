[1] With a change of government in Indonesia in 1998 , supporters of independence for East Timor called for a referendum to decide the territory 's future . [2] Indonesia offered only a degree of autonomy under Indonesian sovereignty . [3] Indonesia 's claims of troop withdrawals from East Timor were contradicted by leaked official documents . [4] Violence increased with East Timorese attacking Indonesian soldiers and mass executions of East Timorese by Indonesian forces . [5] Portugal sided with East Timor . [6] A U.N. envoy mediating the dispute appeared optimistic about a plan for broad autonomy for East Timor under Indonesian sovereignty , [7] but as 1998 moved towards a close the issue was not resolved .
