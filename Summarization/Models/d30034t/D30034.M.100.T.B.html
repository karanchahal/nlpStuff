[1] ~ Indonesia invaded East Timor in December 1975 , [2] a few months after the departure of Portugal 's colonial administration . [3] It annexed the territory , 1,200 miles east of Jakarta , one month later . [4] The United Nations still considers Portugal the administering power [5] and is sponsoring peace talks between Indonesia and Portugal . [6] Portugal and East Timorese pro-independence groups insist that a referendum be held to decide East Timor 's future . [7] Pro-independence activists and Indonesian soldiers have clashed for years . [8] In 1991 , Indonesian troops massacred at least 50 pro- independence protesters . [9] Portugal is trying to extradite former President Suharto for alleged human rights abuses [10] committed under his authoritarian rule .
