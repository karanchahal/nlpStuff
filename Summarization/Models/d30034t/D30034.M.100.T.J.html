[1] The East Timor Human Rights Center accused the Indonesian military of executing 50 East Timorese , possibly in retaliation for an attack on the troops by rebels [2] in which three Indonesian soldiers died . [3] The Indonesian Human Rights Commission said it would investigate the incident . [4] Indonesian President Habibie proposed granting some autonomy to East Timor , [5] which it invaded in 1975 [6] and later annexed , in exchange for its recognition of Indonesian sovereignty . [7] Independence activists have rejected the offer [8] and call for a referendum . [9] The U.N. never recognized Indonesian rule over East Timor [10] and is mediating talks between Indonesia and Portugal , East Timor 's former ruler .
