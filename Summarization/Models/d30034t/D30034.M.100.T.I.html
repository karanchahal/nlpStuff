[1] After several months of relative calm , killing resumed on October 30 , 1998 in the disputed East Timor territory . [2] Three Indonesian soldiers were killed amid rumors [3] that the military was increasing its presence in the territory . [4] Indonesia , which annexed the territory in 1975 , [5] had offered East Timor some autonomy , [6] but would retain control of defense , foreign , and economic policy . [7] The East Timorese were seeking a referendum . [8] The United Nations was trying to mediate an agreement between Portugal and Indonesia . [9] Commemorations of the November 11 , 1991 massacre added to the tensions . [10] Both sides accused the other of mounting new offensives and killings , kidnappings , and demonstrations continued .
