[1] The opening of the Gaza International Airport was stipulated by the U.S.-brokered Wye River accord . [2] Palestinians marked the event with euphoria . [3] The airport will ease the claustrophobia of Gazans [4] and provide a boost to the troubled Palestinian economy . [5] Many Palestinians consider the airport a big step toward independence . [6] Although security remains in Israeli hands , Palestinians will no longer have to travel to Jordan or Israel to travel abroad . [7] Flights are sometimes delayed [8] when the Palestinians and Israelis disagree on security procedures . [9] Since Wye , Israel and Palestine have accused one another of reneging on agreements , [10] and Israel has frozen the accords .
