[1] The Gaza International Airport opened on 24 November 1998 after a delay of two years . [2] The airport was stipulated by the Clinton-brokered Wye River Accords . [3] Jordan , Morocco , Spain and the Netherlands helped finance its construction . [4] Palestinians were elated , considering the airport as a potential economic lifeline , and a step toward statehood . [5] Israel still controls the air space and oversees security . [6] Early operations were disrupted by Israeli-Palestinian security disputes . [7] The first Palestinian commercial flight occurred on 5 December 1998 . [8] The Palestinian Airlines is tiny , [9] but regular flights to Jeddah , Cairo and Amman are planned , [10] and eventually pilgrimage flight to Mecca .
