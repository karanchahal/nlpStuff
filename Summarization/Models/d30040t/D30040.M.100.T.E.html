[1] After a delay of almost two years because of security questions , the Palestinians opened their first international airport in a rare example of cooperation between Israel and Palestine . [2] Israel will continue to share security responsibilities at the airport with the Palestinians . [3] An Egypt Air plane was the first [4] to touch down at the newly opened Gaza International Airport . [5] The highlight , however , was the landing of the first Palestinian Airlines plane . [6] Security concerns continue to plague the running of the airport [7] as Israeli security officials first threatened to close down the airport [8] and later delayed the departure of two planes because of security disputes .
