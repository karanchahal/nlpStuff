[1] Tensions between Turkey and Syria reached a stage of `` all but declared war . '' [2] Turkey accuses Syria of harboring militant Kurdish rebels . [3] Damascus denies sheltering Kurdish fighters . [4] Turkey attacks Kurds in northern Iraq and masses troops on its Syrian border . [5] Syria accuses Turkey of forming military and diplomatic alliances with Israel [6] designed to subvert Syria and Arab states . [7] Further , Syria accuses Turkey of diverting water from rivers that flow through both countries . [8] Israel stresses that it has no part in the Turkey-Syria dispute . [9] Egypt 's President Mubarak with Saudi and Arab support visits both countries to mediate the dispute [10] and defuse tensions .
