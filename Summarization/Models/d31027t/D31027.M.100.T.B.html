[1] Iran 's conservatives won a decisive victory in elections for the 86-member clergy-based Assembly of Experts that oversees the country 's supreme leader . [2] The results were expected [3] since the 12-member Council of Guardians , [4] dominated by hard-liners , [5] had dropped most moderates from the ballot in advance of the election . [6] Iran 's official media boasted about a huge voter turnout , [7] but correspondents , citizens , and others disputed these claims . [8] Many Iranians said they stayed away to protest stacking the deck . [9] The hard-liners won at least 54 places on the 86-seat Assembly and the moderates only 13. Most of the other winners lean toward the hard-line camp .
