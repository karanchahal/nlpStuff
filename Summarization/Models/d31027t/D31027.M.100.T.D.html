[1] Election of members to serve in the Assembly of Experts occurred on 23 October 1998 . [2] The 86-member cleric-based Assembly is important [3] because it names and supervises the country 's supreme leader . [4] The conservatives ' decisive victory was inevitable [5] because the conservative-dominated 12-member Council of Guardians [6] that passed judgment on the list of candidates rejected all except 30 of the moderate candidates . [7] Moderate supporters of President Khatami publicly protested the action of the conservative screening council . [8] Moderates threatened to boycott the election , [9] and voter turnout was unusually low . [10] Conservative hard-liners won at least 54 places on the 86-seat Assembly .
