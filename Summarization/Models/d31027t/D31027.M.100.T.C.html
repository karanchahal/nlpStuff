[1] Iranian pro-democracy demonstrators protested the action of the conservative , 12-member Council of Guardians [2] that rejected 214 of 400 candidates [3] who wished to run in the national election for the 86-member Assembly of Experts . [4] This assembly elects and supervises the country 's supreme leader , [5] who has final say in all things . [6] In vetting these aspirants , [7] the council eliminated most reformist supporters of President Mohammad Khatami , a moderate clergyman with wide popular support . [8] In the heavy boycotted election that followed , [9] hard-liners , not surprisingly , won 54 of the 86 seats ; [10] moderates won 13 , [11] and independents 3. [12] Sixteen seats went to candidates whose affiliation is unclear .
