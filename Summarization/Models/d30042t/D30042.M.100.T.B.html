[1] Two Libyans [2] were indicted in 1991 for blowing up a Pan Am jumbo jet over Lockerbie , Scotland in 1988 . [3] The United Nations imposed sanctions on Libya in 1992 [4] because of their refusal to surrender the suspects . [5] The sanctions included a ban on all international flights . [6] After six years of sanctions , Libyan leader Moammar Gadhafi , faced with threats of additional sanctions , [7] agreed in principle to hand over the suspects . [8] The two suspects would be tried in the Netherlands by Scottish judges under Scottish law . [9] Gadhafi wants guarantees , [10] including a promise that the suspects would serve their sentences in the Netherlands or Libya if convicted .
