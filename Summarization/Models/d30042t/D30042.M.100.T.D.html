[1] In December 1988 a Pan Am jet was blown up over Lockerbie , Scotland , killing 270 . [2] Two Libyan suspects were indicted in 1991 . [3] Since 1992 Libya has been under U.N. sanctions in effect until the suspects are turned over to United States or Britain . [4] Sanctions include an air embargo . [5] African leaders disregard them . [6] Libya has fostered tourism to help the damaged economy . [7] In August 1998 United States and Britain proposed a Netherlands trial . [8] Libya agreed , but then asked for guarantees [9] that the suspects , if convicted , would be incarcerated in Libya . [10] This delayed progress . [11] Kofi Annan planned a December 1988 Libyan trip to move negotiations .
