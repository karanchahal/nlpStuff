[1] For eleven days in September , Hurricane Georges 110-135 mph winds plowed across the Caribbean , [2] killing at least 500 people [3] and inflicting more than $ 5 billion in property damage before expiring over Georgia . [4] The numbers of homeless is staggering . [5] Hard-hit Antigua , Cuba , the Dominican Republic , Puerto Rico and St. Kitts will be hurt further by the continuing loss of tourism . [6] Fortunately , many tourist areas escaped unscathed : the Windward Island group , Trinidad and Tobago , the Dutch Islands , the Bahamas , Cayman Islands , Jamaica and the Turks and Caicos . [7] U.S. aid will be needed for rebuilding , [8] particularly in underinsured Puerto Rico [9] and destitute Haiti .
