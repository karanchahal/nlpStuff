[1] Hurricane Georges , packing winds up to 110 mph , [2] swept through the northern Caribbean [3] killing over 400 people , [4] leaving at least 140,000 homeless , [5] and causing billions in property damage . [6] In Puerto Rico alone , though the residents were forewarned of the hurricane 's approach , [7] nearly 30,000 houses were destroyed . [8] The cost of rebuilding the homes will be largely borne by the residents and U.S. taxpayers , [9] since most homeowners are uninsured . [10] Haiti , the poorest nation in the Western hemisphere was a disaster zone . [11] There , mudslides and floods killed a total of 221 people , 102 in Fonds Verettes . [12] Georges , however , spared many other Caribbean tourist destinations .
