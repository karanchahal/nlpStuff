[1] Hurricane Georges , with winds up to 135 mph , plowed over [2] and past 17 Caribbean islands for 11 days in September 1998 , [3] causing billions of dollars in destruction [4] and killing at least 500 people . [5] The numbers of homeless are staggering , [6] with more than 100,000 people left homeless in the Dominican Republic alone . [7] U.S. taxpayers are sure to foot most of the bill in Puerto Rico , [8] where 30,000 homes were destroyed [9] and another 60,000 damaged . [10] The Caribbean tourist industry 's position remained good [11] since many important tourist destinations were spared . [12] The varied island societies prepared and reacted to Hurricane Georges in radically different ways .
