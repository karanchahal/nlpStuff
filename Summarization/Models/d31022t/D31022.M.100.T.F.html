[1] Over 60 persons , mostly teens , were killed and scores injured in Sweden 's deadliest fire in modern times . [2] The fire occurred in a Goteborg dance hall [3] rented by a Macedonian immigrant group . [4] Over 400 persons crowded [5] the building approved for only 150 . [6] Most at the Halloween hip-hop party were immigrants , or children of immigrants , from war ravaged or impoverished countries . [7] Most victims choked on smoke and poisonous gasses . [8] Survivors claimed [9] response to the fire was slow [10] but firemen were on the scene in six minutes . [11] Cause of the fire has not been established [12] but there were some hints of electrical problems and arson was also being considered .
