[1] The worst fire in Swedish history occurred on October 29 , 1998 , [2] when a Goteborg dancehall , [3] jammed with teenaged immigrants , [4] burst into flame . [5] By November 2 , the death toll stood at 62 , with 162 injured , [6] 17 of whom were still in intensive care . [7] Officials had not yet determined the cause . [8] Four hundred people had been crowded into a hall [9] approved for only 150 . [10] Fire blocked one exit and the other exit contained furniture . [11] Rescuers took 6 minutes to arrive , [12] after having trouble understanding the accented call for help , [13] but the fire spread very rapidly . [14] The dead included individuals with 19 different nationalities .
