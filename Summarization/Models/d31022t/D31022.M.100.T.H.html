[1] Sweden 's deadliest fire in decades , in an overcrowded discotheque with one available exit , killed 62 and injured 162 . [2] Some jumped from windows . [3] Most victims died from smoke inhalation . [4] Almost all were immigrant teenagers [5] representing 19 nationalities . [6] The fire 's cause remains undetermined . [7] Its speed and a report of teens ejected earlier suggest arson . [8] Evidence also points to a short-circuit . [9] Firemen , accused of slow response , [10] arrived 6 minutes after deciphering a call in poor Swedish . [11] Identifying victims was slow [12] because many were too young for drivers licenses . [13] A memorial was held at Goteborg Cathedral . [14] The hall , rated for 150 but packed with 400 , [15] was rented to Kurdish students who organized the party .
