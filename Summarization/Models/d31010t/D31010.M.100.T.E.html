[1] An arctic cold wave , the worst in 10 years , hit parts of Europe , [2] bringing sub-zero temperatures and killing scores of people . [3] Hardest hit were Poland , Bulgaria , and Romania as well as parts of central and eastern France . [4] In Poland , three weeks of sub-zero temperatures killed at least 85 people in November , 29 more than in all of the previous winter . [5] Most of the victims were homeless [6] whose deaths by exposure were alcohol related . [7] Blizzards and cold temperatures also hit Bulgaria and Romania , [8] stranding hundreds in their cars . [9] Elsewhere , snow blanketed the Italian island of Capri for the first time in 10 years .
