[1] An Arctic cold wave brought unusually cold November weather across Europe . [2] Southern Italy experienced snowfall and even Moscow , noted for cold weather , [3] felt the wrath of the early cold spell . [4] Many deaths were attributed to the cold [5] with Poland apparently the hardest hit . [6] The death toll there was greatest among the homeless , especially those [7] who passed out drinking alcohol . [8] In all , 85 deaths there were due to the cold [9] where temperatures dipped as low as minus 26 degrees Celsius . [10] Frostbite and hypothermia sent many to the hospital . [11] Rail schedules throughout Europe were disrupted , [12] ports were closed [13] and many autos were stranded on snow-covered highways .
