[1] Typhoon Babs hit the Philippine Islands on October 21 , 1998 , with 150 mph winds causing flooding , landsides , and flying debris . [2] Hundreds of thousands had to flee their homes , [3] 156 were killed , [4] and $ 100 million worth of crops were damaged . [5] Parts of the northern and central areas were declared a `` state of calamity '' , [6] and , on October 29 , a fund-raising campaign to aid the victims was begun . [7] After leaving the Philippines , Babs moved over the South China Sea , [8] hitting Taiwan on the 25th with heavy rains and winds [9] and pushing strong winds into Hong Kong . [10] Later in the day , Babs weakened to a severe tropical storm .
