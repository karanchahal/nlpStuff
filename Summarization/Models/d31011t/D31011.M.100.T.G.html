[1] Typhoon Babs slammed into the Philippines in mid-October killing at least 150 people , injuring hundreds , [2] and forcing hundreds of thousands to abandon homes damaged or destroyed by winds , floods , and landslides . [3] Worst hit was a coconut-producing area [4] whose damage toll subsequently caused a significant localized rise in cooking oil prices [5] and contributed to inflationary pressures . [6] Fund-raising campaigns began with a one-year salary donation by the Philippine 's president . [7] The powerful storm also brought torrential rains and landslides to Taiwan [8] where floods forced hundreds from their homes . [9] Hong Kong was lashed with strong winds , forcing authorities to open shelters and close beaches .
