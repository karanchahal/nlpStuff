[1] Supertyphoon Babs , on the heels of Supertyphoon Zeb , brought heavy winds and rain to the Philippines , [2] first hitting Catanduanes Island Thursday and stalling there 12 hours . [3] Landslides and drownings killed at least 156 . [4] 400,000 were driven from their homes . [5] The two typhoons destroyed $ 100 million in crops , including abaca and coconut . [6] President Estrada ordered riverside slums evacuated , [7] facilitated emergency fund use and price freezing , [8] and started a typhoon-victim fund . [9] Heading toward southern China , [10] Babs strengthened to sustained winds of 81 mph and gusts up to 100 mph . [11] It brought torrential rains , flooding , and landslides to Taiwan and strong winds to Hong Kong before weakening to a severe tropical storm Sunday night .
