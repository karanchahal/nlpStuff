[1] Making obvious their intention to suppress the fledging China Democratic Party , [2] Communist officials arrested three of its most prominent leaders , Xu Wenli , Qin Yongmin and Wang Youcai . [3] Colleagues protested [4] and the USA expressed concern , to no avail . [5] China , defending its action , [6] brought all three to trial . [7] Because potential defense lawyers were harassed , all three had to defend themselves . [8] Qin and Wang were charged with inciting subversion , [9] which carries a minimum sentence of five years imprisonment . [10] Xu faces the more serious charge of subverting state power [11] which can bring a life sentence . [12] Chinese President Jiang said multiparty democracy will not be allowed .
