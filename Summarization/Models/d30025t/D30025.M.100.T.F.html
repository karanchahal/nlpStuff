[1] Brazil 's economic woes dominated the political scene [2] as President Cardoso faced re-election . [3] His economic program had led to a reduction of the country 's high inflation rate to near zero . [4] However , trade and budget deficits and the need [5] for tax and budget reforms to curb spending kept the economic picture in serious straits . [6] Cardoso easily won re-election [7] as confidence in his program remained high . [8] The victories of his political rivals in gubernatorial and legislative races left further stern measures in doubt . [9] The nation hoped for a relief package from the International Monetary Fund .
