[1] Fernando Cardoso was re-elected as Brazil 's President on 4 October 1998 . [2] He received slightly more than the majority [3] needed to insure a first round victory . [4] Leftist da Silva received 34 % . [5] There was some illegal late campaigning . [6] Half of the country voted on shoe-sized computers [7] that confused some older voters . [8] Brazil is in dire economic straits , [9] and Cardoso promised an austere program to ensure financial credibility . [10] By slashing spending and raising taxes Cardoso hoped [11] the country would qualify for an IMF loan . [12] Many Cardoso allies were trailing in state level run-off races . [13] Their defeats will hurt Cardoso . [14] Pessimism pervaded the presidential inauguration .
