[2] It exploded , killing the bombers and injuring 24 , [3] before explosives-filled suitcases could be carried into the crowd . [6] Israel 's Cabinet immediately suspended deliberations on the US-brokered Wye Accord , [7] under which Israel would relinquish 13 percent more of the West Bank and Arafat would crack down on terrorists . [8] Many doubted Netanyahu would let the accord be implemented , [9] thus playing into militants ' hands . [10] Arafat and Clinton urged that the attack not derail the accord . [11] This third failure of an attempted attack suggested the crackdown on terrorists had produced results .
