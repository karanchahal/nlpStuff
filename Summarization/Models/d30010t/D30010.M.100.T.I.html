[1] On Friday morning , November 6 , 1998 , a red Fiat blew up at a market in Jerusalem . [2] The two bombers were killed [3] and 21 others injured . [4] Netanyahu immediately suspended discussions on ratifying the Wye Peace Accord , [5] which would turn an additional 13 % of the West Bank over to Arafat . [6] The Islamic Jihad was blamed for the blast , [7] which exploded prematurely [8] saving many lives . [9] President Clinton , Secretary of State Albright , and Egyptian President Mabarak urged the Israelis to continue the ratification process . [10] Arafat condemned the bombing [11] and vowed to find the mastermind , [12] but castigated Netanyahu for using the bombing as an excuse to derail the accord .
