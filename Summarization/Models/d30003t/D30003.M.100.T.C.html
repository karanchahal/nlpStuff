[1] Augusto Pinochet , former Chilean dictator , was arrested in London on 14 October 1998 . [2] Pinochet , 82 , was recovering from surgery . [3] The arrest was in response to an extradition warrant [4] served by Baltasar Garzon , a maverick Spanish judge . [5] Pinochet was charged with murdering thousands , [6] including many Spaniards . [7] Pinochet is awaiting a hearing , his fate in the balance . [8] Chile protested , [9] insisting that Pinochet had diplomatic immunity . [10] Britain disagreed . [11] The reaction of the Chilean people was mixed . [12] American scholars applauded the arrest , [13] saying that it set a precedent for other terrorist dictators . [14] Castro criticized the arrest , [15] and called it unprecedented international meddling .
