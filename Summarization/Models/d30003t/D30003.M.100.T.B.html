[1] British authorities arrested General Augusto Pinochet , in London for back surgery , on an international warrant [2] issued by Spanish magistrate Baltasar Garzon . [3] The Madrid court charged Pinochet , [4] who ruled Chile as a despot for 17 years , [5] with crimes against humanity , [6] including genocide and terrorism , involving the deaths of more than 4,000 people . [7] The Chilean government protested that Pinochet , now a `` senator-for-life , '' has legal immunity , [8] but few in Chilean society protested the arrest . [9] Pinochet 's arrest shows the growing significance of international human-rights law , [10] suggesting that officials accused of atrocities have fewer places to hide these days , [11] even if they are carrying diplomatic passports .
