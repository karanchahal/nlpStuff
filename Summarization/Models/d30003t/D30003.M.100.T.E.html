[1] Former Chilean dictator Gen. Augusto Pinochet was arrested in London on charges of human rights violations . [2] Pinochet , in London for back surgery , was arrested in a Spanish warrant [3] accusing him of genocide and terrorism . [4] It is alleged that Pinochet was responsible for the disappearance or death of over 3,000 people [5] while he was dictator of Chile between 1973 and 1900 . [6] Chile protested the arrest [7] stating that Pinochet 's status as senator-for-life gave him diplomatic immunity . [8] Britain 's Labor government was obviously embarrassed by the diplomatic implications of the arrest . [9] The arrest shows the growing significance of international human rights law .
