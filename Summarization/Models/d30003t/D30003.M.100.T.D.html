[1] Augusto Pinochet , former Chilean dictator , was arrested in London on 14 October 1998 . [2] Pinochet , 82 , was recovering from surgery . [3] The arrest was in response to an extradition warrant served by Baltasar Garzon , a maverick Spanish judge . [4] Pinochet was charged with murdering thousands , including many Spaniards . [5] Pinochet is awaiting a hearing , his fate in the balance . [6] Chile protested , [7] insisting that Pinochet had diplomatic immunity . [8] Britain disagreed . [9] The reaction of the Chilean people was mixed . [10] American scholars applauded the arrest , [11] saying that it set a precedent for other terrorist dictators . [12] Castro criticized the arrest , and called it unprecedented international meddling .
