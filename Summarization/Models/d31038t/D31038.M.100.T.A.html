[1] Terrorist bombings of the U.S. Embassies in Nairobi , Kenya and Dar es Salaam , Tanzania on August 7 1998 killed 248 in Nairobi , and 11 in Dar es Salaam . [2] In Nairobi 5400 were injured and 97 fully or partially blinded . [3] In the 76 days following the bombings evidence was gathered linking the perpetrators to the Al Qaeda organization of Osama bin Laden , [4] several indictments were made [5] and several suspects were arrested [6] but no one had yet been brought to trial . [7] A key figure in the Nairobi bombing , Abdallah Mohammed Fazul , head of Al Qaeda in Kenya , had disappeared [8] and was still at large .
