[1] On Pope John Paul II 's three-day visit to Croatia , [2] he beatified controversial cardinal Alojzije Stepinac , [3] in Marija Bistrica , [4] Croatia 's leading shrine to the Virgin Mary . [5] Roman Catholics hail Stepinac as a martyr , [6] but many Serbs and Jews accuse him of sympathizing with the Nazis . [7] The Croats , most of whom are Catholic , [8] welcomed the pope with joy , [9] as did President Franjo Tudjman . [10] At a mass in Split , John Paul called for reconciliation throughout the former Yugoslavia [11] and urged the world to do more for the victims of the Balkan war that ended not long ago in Croatia [12] and that has most recently raged in Kosovo .
