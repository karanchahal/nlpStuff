[1] Pope John Paul II , during a visit to Croatia , beatified the WW II cardinal of Zagreb , Alojzije Stepinac , for his staunch opposition to communism . [2] Though a hero to Catholics , [3] who comprise 80 percent of Croatia , Serbs view Stepinac as a wartime sympathizer with the pro-Nazi dictatorship [4] that governed Croatia and killed thousands of Serbs , Jews , and Gypsies . [5] Stepinac eventually denounced the atrocities , [6] but to Serbs he is a symbol of Vatican cooperation with fascist leaders [7] who are now being recast as Croatian patriots . [8] Arrested in 1946 as a Nazi sympathizer , [9] he was jailed [10] and then released under house arrest in 1951 .
