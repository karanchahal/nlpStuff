[1] Dr. Barnett Slepian was murdered in his suburban Buffalo home on 23 October 1998 . [2] He maintained a private obstetrics practice , [3] and worked regularly at Buffalo 's only public abortion clinic . [4] Snipers had attacked five abortion providers in New York and Canada over the past four years . [5] Slepian had been warned that he was endangered . [6] Abortion rights advocates decried Slepian 's murder , [7] and blamed opponents , including some politicians , of creating a climate that led to the killing . [8] Slepian 's murder was a factor in the New York Attorney General and senatorial campaigns . [9] Investigators reported little progress . [10] The FBI was aiding local police .
